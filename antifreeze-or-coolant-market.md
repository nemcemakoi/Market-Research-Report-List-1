<p><p>Antifreeze or coolant is a liquid substance used in internal combustion engines to regulate temperature and prevent overheating. It is typically a mixture of water and additives, such as ethylene glycol or propylene glycol, which lower the freezing point and raise the boiling point of the liquid. This ensures optimal engine performance in extreme temperatures, prevents corrosion, and extends the lifespan of engine components. Coolants are available in various formulations, including organic acid technology (OAT), inorganic additive technology (IAT), and hybrid organic acid technology (HOAT), catering to different vehicle requirements.</p><p>The Antifreeze or Coolant Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing vehicle production, rising demand for high-performance engines, and the need for efficient thermal management systems. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 11.8% during the forecast period. Key trends include the development of eco-friendly, biodegradable coolants to meet stringent environmental regulations and the growing adoption of electric vehicles, which require specialized thermal management solutions. Additionally, advancements in coolant technology, such as extended-life formulations, are gaining traction. Emerging markets in Asia-Pacific and Latin America are also contributing to market expansion due to rising automotive sales and industrialization. Overall, the antifreeze or coolant market is poised for robust growth, supported by technological innovations and increasing automotive demand.</p></p>
