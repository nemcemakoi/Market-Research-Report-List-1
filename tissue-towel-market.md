<p><p>The tissue towel market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into roll towels, single-folded towels, and multi-folded towels. Roll towels are continuous sheets wound on a sturdy core, ideal for high-traffic areas. Single-folded towels are flat, easier to store, and suited for low-volume use, often found in restrooms. Multi-folded towels consist of individual sheets that are stacked and often dispense one at a time, promoting hygiene and reducing waste. Each type caters to distinct consumer needs and preferences in commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
