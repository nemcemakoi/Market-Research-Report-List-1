<p><p>Utility drones can be categorized into two main types: multi-rotor and fixed-wing. Multi-rotor drones are equipped with multiple rotors, allowing for stable flight and vertical takeoff and landing, making them ideal for tasks like surveying, agriculture, and inspections in confined spaces. In contrast, fixed-wing drones have a traditional airplane structure, enabling longer flight durations and greater coverage areas, making them suitable for extensive mapping and monitoring tasks. Both types enhance efficiency across various industries, including agriculture, construction, and emergency response.</p></p>
