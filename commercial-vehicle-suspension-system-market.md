<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Suspension System</li><li>Semi-Active Suspension System</li><li>Active Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle suspension system market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Passive Suspension System**: This traditional system relies on mechanical components like springs and dampers, providing a fixed level of comfort and stability without active adjustments.</p><p>2. **Semi-Active Suspension System**: This system uses sensors to adjust damping characteristics in real-time, improving ride comfort and handling by adapting to varying road conditions.</p><p>3. **Active Suspension System**: This advanced technology employs actuators to continuously adjust the suspension, offering optimal comfort and performance by responding dynamically to road irregularities.</p></p>
