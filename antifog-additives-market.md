<p><p>The antifog additives market includes glycerol esters, polyglycerol esters, sorbitan esters of fatty acids, and ethoxylated sorbitan esters. Glycerol esters are widely used for their ability to reduce surface tension, preventing fog formation. Polyglycerol esters offer enhanced thermal stability and compatibility with various polymers. Sorbitan esters of fatty acids provide excellent antifogging properties in food packaging. Ethoxylated sorbitan esters are water-soluble and effective in high-humidity environments. These additives are essential in agriculture, food packaging, and other industries to maintain clarity and prevent condensation.</p></p>
