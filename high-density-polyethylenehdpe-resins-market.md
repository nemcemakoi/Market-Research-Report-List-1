<p><p>The High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) resins market is segmented into two main types: adhesive type HDPE resin and coating type HDPE resin. Adhesive type HDPE resins are utilized primarily in bonding applications due to their strong adhesion properties, making them ideal for packaging and construction materials. Conversely, coating type HDPE resins are designed to provide protective and decorative finishes, enhancing durability and appearance for various surfaces. Both types cater to diverse industries, including packaging, automotive, and home goods.</p></p>
