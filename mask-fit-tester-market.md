<p><ul><li>Ambient Aerosol</li><li>Controlled Negative Pressure</li><li>Generated Aerosol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Fit Tester market is categorized into three main types: Ambient Aerosol, Controlled Negative Pressure, and Generated Aerosol. Ambient Aerosol testers assess fit by using particles found in the environment, ensuring real-world applicability. Controlled Negative Pressure testers create a sealed environment to measure the mask's effectiveness in preventing contamination. Generated Aerosol testers introduce specific aerosolized particles in a controlled setting, allowing precise measurement of mask fit and efficacy. Each type serves to ensure safety and compliance in various industries.</p></p>
