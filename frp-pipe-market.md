<p><p>FRP (Fiber Reinforced Plastic) pipes are composite materials made from a polymer matrix reinforced with fibers, typically glass. FRP pipes are lightweight, easy to install, and offer excellent performance in harsh environments, which has driven their adoption globally.</p><p>The FRP Pipe Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period.
