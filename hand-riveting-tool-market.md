<p><p>The hand riveting tool market comprises various types, prominently featuring lever riveters and manually activated rivet guns. Lever riveters utilize a mechanical lever system to efficiently compress and secure rivets, making them ideal for robust applications. Conversely, manually activated rivet guns operate through direct user force, providing flexibility and control for lighter tasks. Both tools serve essential roles in manufacturing and repair industries, catering to different project requirements based on rivet size, material, and ease of use.</p></p>
