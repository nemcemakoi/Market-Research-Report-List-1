<p><p>Insulated Glazing (IG) refers to multi-layered glass units designed to improve thermal insulation in buildings and vehicles by reducing heat transfer. Typically consisting of two or more glass panes separated by a spacer and filled with inert gases, IG enhances energy efficiency, leading to reduced heating and cooling costs. This makes it a preferred choice in residential, commercial, and automotive applications.</p><p>The Insulated Glazing (IG) Market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period.
