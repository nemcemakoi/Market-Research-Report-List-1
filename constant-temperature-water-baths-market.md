<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Baths</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Baths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant temperature water baths are essential in laboratories for maintaining specific temperature conditions. The market consists of two primary types: circulating and non-circulating water baths. Circulating water baths utilize pumps to ensure even temperature distribution throughout the water, making them ideal for precise heating applications. In contrast, non-circulating water baths maintain a static water level, providing simpler and more cost-effective solutions, suitable for less demanding temperature control tasks. Both types cater to various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
