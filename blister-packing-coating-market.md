<p><p>The blister packing coating market comprises two primary types: solvent-based and water-based coatings. Solvent-based blister packing coatings utilize organic solvents to dissolve the coating materials, offering excellent adhesion and versatility but raising concerns about VOC emissions. Conversely, water-based blister packing coatings use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower VOC levels, although they may have specific application limitations. Both types serve crucial roles in protecting products from moisture and external factors during storage and transport.</p></p>
