<p><p>Fastener covers are protective coatings applied to fasteners to enhance durability, corrosion resistance, and aesthetics. **Cadmium** offers excellent corrosion resistance and lubricity but is less environmentally friendly. **Black Oxide** provides a sleek finish and mild corrosion resistance, often used for appearance. **Zinc** coatings, like galvanizing, are cost-effective and widely used for rust prevention. **Nickel** offers high corrosion resistance and a polished look. **Silver** is rare, used for conductivity and high-temperature applications. **Phosphate** improves adhesion and wear resistance, often used with oils for added protection.</p></p>
