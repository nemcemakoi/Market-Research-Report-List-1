<p><strong>The In-mold Labeling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding Type</li><li>Blow molding Type</li><li>Thermoforming Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-mold labeling machine market consists of three primary types: Injection Molding Type, Blow Molding Type, and Thermoforming Type. </p><p>Injection Molding Type uses high-pressure injections to create complex shapes while embedding labels directly into the product. </p><p>Blow Molding Type employs air pressure to expand molten plastic into a desired shape, also integrating labels during the forming process. </p><p>Thermoforming Type involves heating plastic sheets, then molding them around labels, allowing for versatile packaging solutions. Each type offers unique advantages for various applications.</p></p>
