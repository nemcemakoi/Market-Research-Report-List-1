<p><p>Cast Polypropylene (CPP) films are versatile packaging materials known for their clarity, strength, and moisture resistance. In the CPP films market, two main types are prevalent: clear films and opaque films. Clear CPP films are transparent, ideal for showcasing products while providing protection, making them popular in sectors like food packaging and retail. Opaque CPP films, on the other hand, offer light-blocking properties, making them suitable for packaging applications that require protection from light and UV exposure, like pharmaceuticals and processed foods.</p></p>
