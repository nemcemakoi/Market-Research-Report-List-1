<p><p>Ultra-Low Refrigerated-Heating Circulators are classified into three market types based on their circulation methods. </p><p>Internal Circulation features pumps that circulate fluid solely within the unit, enhancing temperature control for smaller applications. </p><p>External Circulation allows fluid to be circulated out of the unit to external equipment, providing versatile cooling for larger setups. </p><p>Internal-External Circulation combines both methods, enabling fluid to circulate internally and externally, which offers flexibility and precise temperature management for complex experimental setups and industrial processes.</p></p>
