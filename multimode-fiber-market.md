<p><p>The multimode fiber market comprises several types, including OM1, OM2, OM3, OM4, and others. OM1 supports lower bandwidths with a core diameter of 62.5 microns, ideal for short distances. OM2, with a 50-micron core, offers improved performance up to 500 meters. OM3 and OM4 fibers, also 50 microns, provide higher bandwidth capabilities, suitable for data centers, with OM4 supporting distances up to 150 meters at 100 Gbps. The "Others" category includes emerging and specialized multimode fibers for specific applications.</p></p>
