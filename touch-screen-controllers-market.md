<p><ul><li>Resistive Touch Controllers</li><li>Capacitive Touch Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen controller market is primarily divided into two types: resistive and capacitive touch controllers. Resistive touch controllers operate by detecting pressure applied on a flexible surface, making them ideal for environments where moisture or gloves are used. In contrast, capacitive touch controllers rely on the electrical properties of the human body, offering higher sensitivity and multi-touch capabilities, which enhance user experience. Each type caters to different applications, ranging from industrial devices to smartphones and tablets.</p></p>
