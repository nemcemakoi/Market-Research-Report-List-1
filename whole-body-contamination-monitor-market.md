<p><strong>The Whole Body Contamination Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Scintillation Detector</li><li>Gas Flow Proportional Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Whole Body Contamination Monitors primarily use two types of detection technologies: Plastic Scintillation Detectors and Gas Flow Proportional Detectors. Plastic Scintillation Detectors utilize plastic scintillator materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, allowing for efficient detection of contaminants on the body. In contrast, Gas Flow Proportional Detectors measure radiation by ionizing gas within a chamber, generating an electrical signal proportional to the radiation level. Both technologies play crucial roles in ensuring radiation safety in various environments, including healthcare and nuclear industries.</p></p>
