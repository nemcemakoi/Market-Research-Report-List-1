<p><p>The Heavy Duty Diesel (HDD) catalysts market encompasses various emission control technologies designed to reduce harmful pollutants. Hydrocarbons (HC) catalysts target unburned fuel emissions, promoting cleaner combustion. Carbon Monoxide (CO) catalysts facilitate the oxidation of carbon monoxide into less harmful carbon dioxide. Oxides of Nitrogen (NOx) catalysts, such as Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR), convert NOx gases into nitrogen and water. Particulate Matter (PM) catalysts focus on capturing and oxidizing soot particles, enhancing air quality and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
