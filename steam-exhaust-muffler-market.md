<p><p>The steam exhaust muffler market features two primary types: outlet steam exhaust mufflers and inlet steam exhaust mufflers. Outlet steam exhaust mufflers are designed to reduce noise and pressure in steam systems as steam exits, ensuring efficient operation and compliance with noise regulations. Inlet steam exhaust mufflers, on the other hand, are installed at the entry point of steam systems to mitigate noise generated during steam intake. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing operational efficiency and reducing environmental impact in industrial applications.</p></p>
