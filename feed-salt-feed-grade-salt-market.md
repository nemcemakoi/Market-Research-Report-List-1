<p><p>The Feed Salt market comprises various types, primarily categorized into Sea Salt, Well and Rock Salt, and Lake Salt. Sea Salt is harvested through evaporation of seawater, offering essential minerals beneficial for livestock. Well and Rock Salt is mined from underground deposits and provides a concentrated source of sodium. Lake Salt is obtained from evaporated saltwater lakes, rich in trace elements. Together, these types fulfill the nutritional and regulatory requirements of animal feed, supporting livestock health and growth.</p></p>
