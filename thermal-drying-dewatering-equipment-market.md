<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal drying dewatering equipment is essential for removing moisture from sludge and waste materials. The **direct type** utilizes direct contact between hot air or gas and the material, allowing for efficient moisture evaporation but may affect the material’s quality. In contrast, the **indirect type** heats the material without direct contact, using heated surfaces, which safeguards material integrity and is suitable for sensitive applications. Both types serve specific industrial needs, optimizing the drying process based on desired outcomes.</p></p>
