<p><p>The signal converter market primarily consists of two key types: Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADCs) and Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs). ADCs transform analog signals, such as sound or temperature, into digital form for processing by computers and digital devices, enabling more precise data manipulation. Conversely, DACs convert digital signals back into analog form, allowing devices to produce sound or display visual information. Both types are essential in various applications, including consumer electronics, telecommunications, and industrial automation systems.</p></p>
