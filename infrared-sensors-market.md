<p><p>The infrared sensors market primarily includes thermal and quantum sensors. Thermal infrared sensors detect emitted heat from objects, making them ideal for applications like thermal imaging and temperature measurement. They rely on the temperature-dependent changes in their materials. Quantum infrared sensors, on the other hand, utilize quantum mechanical principles to detect infrared radiation more sensitively, often employed in spectroscopy and gas detection. Each type serves distinct applications, highlighting the diverse needs within the infrared sensing landscape.</p></p>
