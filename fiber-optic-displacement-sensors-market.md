<p><ul><li>Intensity Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li><li>Phase Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li><li>Wavelength Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li><li>Polarization Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic displacement sensors are categorized into four primary types based on modulation techniques. Intensity modulated fiber optic sensors detect changes in light intensity to measure displacement. Phase modulated sensors utilize phase shifts in light to provide precise measurements. Wavelength modulated sensors analyze variations in light wavelengths for displacement detection, while polarization modulated sensors focus on changes in the polarization state of light. Each type offers unique advantages, making them suitable for various applications in industries like manufacturing, aerospace, and civil engineering.</p></p>
