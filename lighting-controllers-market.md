<p><p>The lighting controllers market is divided into two primary types: wired and wireless lighting controllers. Wired lighting controllers utilize physical connections like cables to manage and control lighting systems, offering reliability and stability. These systems are typically more complex to install but are ideal for large installations. In contrast, wireless lighting controllers operate through radio signals, allowing for flexible installation and remote control. They are popular in residential and commercial spaces for their convenience and ease of integration into smart home systems.</p></p>
