<p><p>The Digital Variable Gain Amplifier (DVGA) market includes three primary types: voltage gain, current gain, and power gain. Voltage gain amplifiers adjust output voltage relative to input voltage, primarily used in audio and signal processing applications. Current gain amplifiers enhance the current flowing through a circuit, suitable for low-power devices. Power gain amplifiers increase the output power, critical in RF and communication systems for maintaining signal integrity. Each type serves distinct applications, driven by performance requirements.</p></p>
